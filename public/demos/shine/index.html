Our Story

Where It Began

Shine was born from a simple conviction: coffee deserves the same reverence as any fine craft. Our founders spent years traveling through the highlands of Ethiopia, the volcanic slopes of Colombia, and the rich valleys of Brazil, learning from farmers who have tended their land for generations.

What they found was a world of quiet dedication — hands sorting cherries at dawn, families passing down knowledge through generations, and a universal belief that great coffee begins with respect for the land. Shine is the culmination of those journeys, a roastery built on relationships and a relentless pursuit of the perfect cup.

Hands sorting coffee cherries
Coffee cherries on branch

Harvest

Every journey begins at origin. We work directly with farming communities who hand-pick only the ripest cherries, ensuring that each bean carries the full expression of its terroir. The harvest is a meditation — slow, deliberate, and guided by decades of intuition.

Beans mid-roast in drum roaster

Roasting

In the roastery, silence is our most important tool. We listen for the first crack, watch for the shift in color, and feel the moment when raw potential transforms into something transcendent. Every roast is a performance — no two are exactly alike.

Pour-over mid-stream, steam rising

Brewing

The brew is where science meets soul. Water temperature, grind size, extraction time — each variable is a note in a larger composition. We test every batch through multiple brew methods to unlock its fullest potential before it reaches your hands.

Finished cup on wooden counter

Serving

The final moment — when steam rises from the cup and the first aroma reaches you. This is the shine. Everything before this was preparation; this is revelation. We roast so that this moment, your moment, is extraordinary every single time.

“ We don't just roast coffee. We listen to it. Every bean has a story — our job is to let it speak. — The Founders

Our Commitments

Every decision we make is guided by a set of principles that honor the craft, the community, and the planet. From direct trade relationships to carbon-neutral operations, we believe that excellence and responsibility are inseparable.
